Guacamole

Anyone who has visited Mexico or been to an authentic Mexican restaurant will agree
that there is guacamole…then there are really good guacamoles.

Guacamole Ingredients

  • Limes
  • Jalapeno
  • Salt
  • Red onion
  • Avocados
  • Coriander or cilantro

Instructions to make

  1. Place the onion, jalapeño,
    salt and half the coriander
    on a cutting board and use
    a fork to mash until juicy.
    OR do this in a mortar and
    pestle - grind into a paste.
  2. Scrape into a bowl, add
    avocado and remaining
    coriander, and mash to
    desired consistency.
  3. Do a taste test then adjust
    to your taste: salt, lime juice
    for sour, more chilli for
    spiciness.
  4. If using tomatoes, stir through.
  5. Serve with corn chips!
